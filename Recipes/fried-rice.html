Fried Rice Recipe

Fried Rice is a common Chinese dish of cooked rice that has been stir-fried in a frying pan. This recipe is a family recipe that puts a spin on fried rice, such that there is only four required ingredients.


Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons butter, divided
  • 2 eggs whisked
  • 1/2 cup frozen peas
  • salt and black pepper
  • 2 green onions, sliced
  • 4 slices of bacon
  • 1/2 cup of sliced ham, in pieces
  • 4 cups of cooked, room temperature jasmine rice

Instructions

  1. Heat a large frying pan over medium heat
  2. Place the bacon and ham in the pan, flip every two minutes
  3. After bacon and ham is fully cooked, take both out of pan and slice into pieces
  4. Return the bacon and ham back to the pan, along with butter, peas, and green onion
  5. Let the mixture cook on low for one minute
  6. Add in rice to pan and stir to combine
