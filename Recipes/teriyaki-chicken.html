Teriyaki Chicken Recipe

This recipe of Teriyaki Chicken is a recipe from my father and grandfather. This is a 8-12 hour marinated teryaki chicken recipe, in which was passed down in a special way. I was only told the ingredients in the recipe and not the increments of each ingredient, something I was to find out for myself.


Ingredients

  • 2.5 cups of soy sauce
  • 3 cloves of garlic
  • 1 package of bone-in chicken thighs
  • black pepper
  • 2 green onions, sliced
  • 1 gallon ziploc bag

Instructions

  1. Take out chicken from package and wash and place to the side to dry
  2. Slice the green onions into pieces and place to the side
  3. Peel the garlic, and flatten each one individually until you see creases in garlic
  4. Add the soy sauce, garlic, green onion, and pepper to the ziploc bag
  5. Add rinsed chicken thighs to bag and let marinade for 8-12 hours
  6. After 8-12 hours, grill chicken over medium to medium high-heat for 3 minutes per side
  7. Flip chicken at least twice
