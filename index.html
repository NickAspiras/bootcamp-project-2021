Recipe List

Fried Rice

Fried Rice is a common Chinese dish of cooked rice that has been stir-fried in a frying pan. This recipe is a family recipe that puts a spin on fried rice, such that there is only four required ingredients.

Chicken Katsu

This is Chicken Katsu, a Japanese dish of fired chicken coated in panko bread crumbs. Chicken katsu is generally served with shredded cabbage, rice and tonkatsu sauce, a thick vegetarian pureed sauce.

Teriyaki Chicken

This recipe of Teriyaki Chicken is a recipe from my father and grandfather. This is a 8-12 hour marinated teryaki chicken recipe, in which was passed down in a special way. I was only told the ingredients in the recipe and not the increments of each ingredient, something I was to find out for myself.
